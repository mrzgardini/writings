Wolfram Data Science Boot Camp 2024 https://community.wolfram.com/groups/-/m/t/3188040

Join the two-week online boot camp, to connect remotely with Wolfram instructors for a guided online introduction to applied multiparadigm data science. You will have the option to use either your own dataset at camp or one of the curated datasets from the Wolfram Data Repository or the built-in knowledgebase in the Wolfram Language, to discover insights and develop results in an area that is of importance to you.
